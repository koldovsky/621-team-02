About us

Established in 2015, Runnerz is a global non-profit organization founded by pioneer runner Mark Kelz. We use running as a vehicle to empower and unite people through the creation of local running clubs, education programs, communication platforms, and social running events.
We aim to break down the barriers of geography and create a global community for runners of all abilities to support, encourage, and inspire each other towards a positive sense of self and fearlessness. RunnerZ can be found currently in 5 states and there will be more soon.
